# Label Definition Review: gilmorebykovskyi2018_ci_ehr_phenotype

## Review Status

Complete.

## Label Summary

Dementia status was identified using validated Medicare ICD-9 claims codes; narrative statements were then classified into cognitive and behavioral symptom categories.

## Label Type

Claims-identified dementia cohort plus manual classification of extracted narrative statements.

## Target Construct

Symptoms of cognitive impairment and behavioral dysfunction in known dementia patients.

## Timing

Unstructured documentation from the last three days of hospitalization.

## Strengths

Classification criteria were expert-informed and reliability was reported.

## Weaknesses

No matched controls; no gold-standard cognitive assessment; dementia and delirium could not be separated.

## Risk of Label Leakage

Not applicable for prediction. The study intentionally reviews notes from known dementia patients to derive terminology.

## Use in Review

Use for terminology development and phenotype feature design, not outcome validation.
