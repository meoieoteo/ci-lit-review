# Validation Review: gilmorebykovskyi2018_ci_ehr_phenotype

## Review Status

Complete.

## Validation Design

Reliability validation of manual statement classification through 10% random re-review.

## Metrics

Overall agreement 85.8%, Cohen kappa 0.82, and Gwet AC1 0.83.

## Main Results

Most records contained at least one cognitive-impairment symptom statement, and classification reliability was high.

## Strengths

The reliability assessment supports the consistency of the symptom-category taxonomy.

## Limitations

No predictive validation, no control group, no external validation, no sensitivity/specificity for detecting cognitive impairment.

## Transportability

Uncertain. Two hospital systems/EHRs were included, but local terminology may differ.

## Use in Review

Use reliability evidence for terminology derivation only.
