## One-Sentence Takeaway

Mattke et al. shows that Medicare claims document only a small fraction of expected MCI cases, making absence of MCI codes a poor negative label.

## Bottom Line for This Literature Review

## Label, Target, and Timing Takeaways

## Validation and Transportability Takeaways

## Bias and Downstream-Action Takeaways

## Implications for Study Design

Use indeterminate label handling, minimum follow-up/opportunity criteria, and sensitivity analyses for future diagnosis labels.

## Claims to Carry Forward

## Claims to Treat Cautiously

Exact true prevalence and exact overdiagnosis/underdetection estimates.
