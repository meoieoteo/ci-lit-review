# Specialist Review: Label Definition

## Specialist Scope

Mental health crisis relapse label.

## Bottom Line

The label is a care-event outcome and is indirect for dementia work.

## Label Summary

Binary crisis event within the next 28 days at weekly prediction points.

## Target Construct

`future_care_pathway`; `other`

## Label Source and Operational Definition

EHR-derived mental health crisis events.

## Index Date and Label Timing

Weekly time origin with 28-day follow-up.

## Positive Case Definition

Crisis event/relapse in the horizon.

## Negative or Control Definition

No crisis event in the horizon.

## Indeterminate or Excluded Cases

Not central to the dementia review.

## Label Validity Evidence

Outcome validity should be interpreted within mental-health service context.

## Misclassification Risks

Events outside captured services may be missed.

## Downstream-Action and Diagnosis-Opportunity Bias

The outcome depends on crisis-service contact and documentation.

## Leakage Risks

Rolling prediction requires strict exclusion of future notes; source appears designed around weekly prediction.

## Fit to Pre-Cardiology Risk Scoring

Indirect but useful for thinking about rolling horizons.

## Strengths

Explicit short prediction horizon.

## Concerns

Care-event label, not disease-state label.

## Missing Information

Not reviewed deeply for dementia label design.

## Implications for This Literature Review

Use as analogy for horizon setup and care-event bias.

## Recommended Follow-up

Do not use for dementia label claims.
