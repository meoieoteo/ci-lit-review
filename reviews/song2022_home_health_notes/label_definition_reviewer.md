# Specialist Review: Label Definition

## Specialist Scope

Hospitalization or ED visit during home health care.

## Bottom Line

The outcome is operationally clear but is a utilization/care-process label, not a disease-state label.

## Label Summary

Binary hospitalization or ED visit during HHC episode.

## Target Construct

`future_care_pathway`; `other`

## Label Source and Operational Definition

Outcome extracted from OASIS.

## Index Date and Label Timing

Home-health episode is the time origin.

## Positive Case Definition

Hospitalization or ED visit during the episode.

## Negative or Control Definition

No hospitalization/ED visit during the episode.

## Indeterminate or Excluded Cases

Not central to current dementia review.

## Label Validity Evidence

OASIS outcome capture is plausible but not independently reviewed here.

## Misclassification Risks

Events outside captured documentation may be missed.

## Downstream-Action and Diagnosis-Opportunity Bias

Strong, because utilization depends on access, clinician decisions, and patient behavior.

## Leakage Risks

Notes from within the episode may be close to or after deterioration unless timing is tightly controlled.

## Fit to Pre-Cardiology Risk Scoring

Indirect analogy only.

## Strengths

Clear episode-level outcome.

## Concerns

Care-utilization label and possible within-episode timing leakage.

## Missing Information

Exact temporal cutoff for note inclusion before outcome.

## Implications for This Literature Review

Use to illustrate care-process labels and note-feature gains.

## Recommended Follow-up

Do not generalize to dementia disease-state labels.
