# Consolidated Review: song2022_home_health_notes

## Consolidation Status

consolidated_complete

## One-Sentence Takeaway

Song 2022 shows that home-health clinical notes can improve hospitalization/ED prediction, making it a useful but indirect structured-plus-text analogy.

## Bottom Line for This Literature Review

Use for the general claim that note-derived features can add predictive signal beyond structured EHR/assessment data.

## What This Paper Should Be Cited For

`ehr_text_signal`; `note_concept_extraction`; `validation_quality`; `background_only`

## What This Paper Should Not Be Cited For

Do not cite for dementia prediction, cardiology workflows, or disease-state labels.

## Publication Status and Evidence Weight

Peer-reviewed article with indirect evidence weight for this review.

## Evidence Strength

`useful_analogy`; `background_context`

## Key Contributions

Large HHC episode sample, structured-only versus note-augmented comparisons, PRC/F-score reporting, and interpretable note features.

## Key Limitations

Non-dementia endpoint, care-utilization label, no external validation, HHC-specific features.

## Agreements Across Reviewers

All reviewers agree it is an analogy for additive note value rather than dementia evidence.

## Tensions or Disagreements Across Reviewers

None.

## Label, Target, and Timing Takeaways

Utilization labels are care-process labels. Episode timing may create leakage unless controlled.

## Validation and Transportability Takeaways

Internal validation supports the paper's own task; portability is untested.

## Bias and Downstream-Action Takeaways

Hospitalization/ED use reflects access and clinician decisions.

## Implications for Study Design

Compare structured-only, note-only, and structured-plus-note models; report PRC metrics for imbalanced outcomes.

## Claims to Carry Forward

Clinical notes can improve risk prediction when added to structured data.

## Claims to Treat Cautiously

HHC hospitalization prediction does not imply dementia detection performance.

## Open Questions

Which note-derived functional or social-risk concepts could transfer to cognitive-care models?

## Recommended Next Action

Use as indirect support for additive text signal.
