# Consolidated Review: penfold2022_mci_nlp

## Consolidation Status

consolidated_complete

## One-Sentence Takeaway

Penfold 2022 shows that cognitive concepts extracted from primary-care notes can identify some MCI cases, but its pilot model is too insensitive for broad screening.

## Bottom Line for This Literature Review

Use this as direct evidence that routine notes contain cognitive signals and as a caution that interpretable concept extraction alone may produce low-recall models.

## What This Paper Should Be Cited For

`ehr_text_signal`; `note_concept_extraction`; `label_construction`; `validation_quality`

## What This Paper Should Not Be Cited For

Do not cite it as evidence for a clinically ready cardiology risk score, external transportability, or high-sensitivity screening.

## Publication Status and Evidence Weight

Peer-reviewed direct evidence, with moderate-to-limited weight because it is a pilot with modest AUC and very low sensitivity at the reported operating point.

## Evidence Strength

`moderate_direct_evidence`; `methodological_caution`

## Key Contributions

Interpretable cognitive concepts, cognitive-test labels, and held-out general-population validation.

## Key Limitations

Selective cognitive testing, no external validation, uncertain cardiology fit, and poor sensitivity.

## Agreements Across Reviewers

All reviewers agree the paper is directly relevant to note-derived cognitive signals but not deployment-ready.

## Tensions or Disagreements Across Reviewers

No major disagreement; the main tension is strong conceptual relevance versus weak operating characteristics.

## Label, Target, and Timing Takeaways

Cognitive test labels are useful but selected. Timing relative to cognitive testing matters for leakage.

## Validation and Transportability Takeaways

Held-out validation is useful; external and specialty transport remain untested.

## Bias and Downstream-Action Takeaways

Testing and documentation may be downstream of concern, producing diagnosis-opportunity bias.

## Implications for Study Design

Include explicit note cutoffs before cardiology encounter start, report sensitivity at useful thresholds, and validate locally.

## Claims to Carry Forward

Routine notes can encode cognitive-function concepts relevant to MCI identification.

## Claims to Treat Cautiously

Concept-extraction models are not automatically useful as screening tools.

## Open Questions

Which concept families improve recall in outpatient cardiology notes?

## Recommended Next Action

Use as a direct concept-inventory source and source-check threshold values before manuscript citation.
