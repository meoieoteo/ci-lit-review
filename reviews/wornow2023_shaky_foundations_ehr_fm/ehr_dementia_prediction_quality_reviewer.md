## Review Status

Complete.

## Dementia-Relevance

Indirect. The paper does not focus on dementia or cognitive impairment.

## Prediction Versus Phenotyping

Not applicable as an original prediction study.

## Clinical Specificity

Low for dementia, high for general clinical ML evaluation.

## Feature Quality

The review is useful because it distinguishes text-based clinical language models from structured longitudinal EHR foundation models and notes that most structured EHR models rely on coded data.

## Model Quality

The paper argues that clinical foundation-model quality cannot be inferred from generic benchmark gains alone.

## Implementation Quality

High relevance. It stresses the need to evaluate workflow capacity, deployment cost, usability, fairness, and automation bias.

## Review Use

Use as a methodological caution citation in the dementia EHR review, especially when discussing why a larger model is not automatically better for pre-encounter cognitive-risk stratification.
