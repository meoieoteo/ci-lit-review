## Bibliographic Record
Liang, Xiaohua, Di Zhang, Huan Wang, Muhammad Fahad Tahir, Lanling Chen, Xiaodong Zhao, and Zhiyong Zou. "Importance of Cohort and Target Trial Emulation in Clinical Research." Cell Reports Medicine 6, no. 4 (2025): 102081. https://doi.org/10.1016/j.xcrm.2025.102081.

## Plain-Language Summary
This commentary explains how cohort studies and target trial emulation support stronger clinical research from observational data. It emphasizes careful study design, standardized data processing, life-course research, data sharing, and integration of population studies with omics.
