# heckerman1991_probabilistic_similarity_networks

## Bibliographic Record
Heckerman, David E. Probabilistic Similarity Networks. MIT Press, 1991. https://arxiv.org/abs/1911.06263.

## Plain-Language Summary
This book develops probabilistic similarity networks, a knowledge-engineering method for constructing probabilistic expert systems. It is grounded in the Pathfinder diagnostic expert system and addresses how to acquire and structure probabilistic knowledge for diagnosis.
