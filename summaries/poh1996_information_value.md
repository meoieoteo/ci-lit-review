# poh1996_information_value

## Bibliographic Record
Poh, Kim Leng, and Eric J. Horvitz. "A Graph-Theoretic Analysis of Information Value." Proceedings of the Twelfth Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence, 1996, 427-435. https://arxiv.org/abs/1302.3596.

## Plain-Language Summary
This paper studies how the structure of an influence diagram can indicate which uncertain variables are more valuable to observe before making a decision. It derives qualitative dominance relations for expected value of information without requiring full numerical computation.
